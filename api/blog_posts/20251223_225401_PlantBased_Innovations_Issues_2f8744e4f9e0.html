Alert: Undeclared Almond in H-E-B Dairy-Free Coconut Yogurt Could Trigger Allergies

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (Youdle) â€“ Heads up, shoppers! PlantBased Innovations has issued an allergy alert for its Higher Harvest by H-E-B Dairy-Free Coconut Yogurt, Strawberry flavor. The yogurt may contain undeclared almond, posing a potential threat to those with allergies or severe sensitivity to almonds.

The affected product was distributed to H-E-B and shipped out on November 24, 2025. It reached grocery stores in Texas via H-E-B distribution centers. If consumed by someone with an almond allergy, it could cause a serious or even life-threatening allergic reaction.

  • Product Name: Higher Harvest by H-E-B Dairy-Free Coconut Yogurt, Strawberry flavor
  • Package: 5.3 oz plastic yogurt cups with foil lids
  • Best By date: January 2, 2026 (01/02/26)

So far, no illnesses or injuries have been reported. If you have the affected product at home, it would be wise to avoid consumption, especially if you're allergic to almonds. Stay safe and stay informed with Youdle.
