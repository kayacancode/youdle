Trader Joe's Gingerbread Ice Cream Sandwiches Send Shoppers Into a Frenzy

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (Youdle) – Trader Joe's has unveiled a new seasonal treat that's sending shoppers into a frenzy. The Gingerbread Ice Cream Sandwiches have become a hot topic on social media, with fans already swooning over the festive dessert.

The 4-count box retails for just $3.99 and the sandwiches, described as "gingerbread cookies with vanilla bean ice cream," are a delightful 170 calories each. The packaging itself, a charming dark green box adorned with images of gingerbread men, adds to the holiday appeal.

Here's what some shoppers on Instagram had to say:

  • "Adorable. Delicious. Dangerous."
  • "Tastes like Christmas met a cozy cookie jar and decided to chill."
  • "My kids will love these!"

Want to try these treats? Some handy tips from the Youdle community include letting them sit out for 2–3 minutes for a chewy texture or adding a festive crunch by rolling the edges in crushed candy canes. Yum!
