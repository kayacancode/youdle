Trader Joe's Gingerbread Ice Cream Sandwiches: The New Frozen Treat Shoppers Love!

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (Youdle) – Trader Joe's is ringing in the holiday spirit with a mouth-watering new addition to their frozen desserts aisle: Gingerbread Ice Cream Sandwiches. Spotted by enthusiastic shoppers on social media, this delectable treat is already causing quite a buzz.

The dessert, retailing at just $3.99 for a 4-count box, features gingerbread cookies filled with vanilla bean ice cream, each offering a sweet 170-calorie indulgence. The charming packaging - a festive dark green box with gingerbread man-shaped ice cream sandwiches sprawled across - makes it even more irresistible.

  • Pro Tip 1: Let them sit out for 2–3 minutes for that perfect chewy-cookie texture.
  • Pro Tip 2: Crumble one into a mug, drizzle caramel or hot fudge for a divine twist.
  • Pro Tip 3: Roll the edges in crushed candy canes or Vanilla Sugar for a festive crunch.

According to one Instagram post, these sandwiches taste like "Christmas met a cozy cookie jar and decided to chill. Literally." Now that's a flavor profile that sounds tempting, doesn't it?
