But not all hope is lost! Trae Bodge, a smart shopping expert, has shared some budget-friendly tips for grocery shopping:

  • Consider eating vegetarian meals a few nights a week. Prices for poultry, meats, and fish have risen more than those of vegetables, rice, and pasta.
  • Plan meals around products that have not seen significant price hikes.
