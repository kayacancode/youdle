Get Ready for a Sweet Holiday Treat: Trader Joe's Gingerbread Ice Cream Sandwiches!

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (Youdle) – Just in time for the holiday season, Trader Joe's has introduced a delightful new frozen dessert. Shoppers are buzzing on social media about the latest addition to the grocery store's lineup: Gingerbread Ice Cream Sandwiches.

The new dessert, according to the product's packaging, is a 4-count seasonal offering retailing for just $3.99. Each Gingerbread Ice Cream Sandwich, described as "gingerbread cookies with vanilla bean ice cream," is a mere 170 calories per serving. Encased in a charming dark green box, these gingerbread man-shaped treats are sure to be a hit with your family and friends this holiday season.

  • Let them sit out for 2–3 minutes to achieve the perfect chewy-cookie texture.
  • Try crumbling one into a mug, drizzling caramel or hot fudge on top for a decadent dessert.
  • Add a festive touch by rolling the edges in crushed candy canes or Vanilla Sugar.

Why not add some holiday cheer to your shopping list with these delightful treats? Remember, they're a seasonal offering, so grab them while you can at your local Trader Joe's. As one Instagram user aptly put it, these treats "taste like Christmas met a cozy cookie jar and decided to chill. Literally."
