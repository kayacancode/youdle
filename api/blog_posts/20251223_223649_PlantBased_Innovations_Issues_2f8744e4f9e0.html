  • Product Name: Higher Harvest by H-E-B Dairy-Free Coconut Yogurt, Strawberry flavor
  • Package: 5.3 oz plastic yogurt cups with foil lids

As of now, there have been no reported illnesses or injuries linked to this product. However, if you've purchased this product and have an almond allergy or sensitivity, it's crucial to avoid consumption.
