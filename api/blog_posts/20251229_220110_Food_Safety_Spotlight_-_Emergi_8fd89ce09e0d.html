Stay Alert: Emerging Food Safety Concerns

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (Youdle) – With rising global threats due to unsafe foods, it's becoming more crucial than ever to be aware of foodborne illnesses. New data highlights the need for cross-border action and unity in the entire food supply chain to ensure our health and safety.

Several studies have recently come into the spotlight, detailing the harm caused by specific foodborne pathogens. These pathogens include:

  • Extended-Spectrum β-lactamase–Producing Klebsiella pneumoniae
  • Listeria monocytogenes
  • Multidrug-Resistant Salmonella enterica
  • OXA-48–Producing Escherichia coli
  • Escherichia coli O157:H7

Understanding these threats can help us make informed decisions about what we put on our tables. Staying informed is a crucial step in protecting our health and wallets from the impacts of foodborne diseases.
