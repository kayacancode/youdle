Smart shopping expert, Trae Bodge, shares her top tips to help you navigate these rising prices. Here are a few:

  • Eat vegetarian: Poultry, meats, and fish prices have surged more than vegetables and pantry staples like rice and pasta. Try incorporating vegetarian meals a few nights a week to cut costs.
  • Plan your meals: Planning meals in advance helps avoid impulsive buying and reduces food wastage.
  • Buy in bulk: Non-perishable items bought in bulk often come cheaper.
