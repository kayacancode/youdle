Beat Inflation: Shop Smart & Save on Your Next Grocery Trip!

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (Youdle) – With consumer prices on a steep climb, up by 6.5% compared to last year, savvy shopping strategies are a must, especially for groceries.

Despite the inflation rate falling for six consecutive months, it's still near a 40-year high. Our grocery bills are feeling the pinch with food prices having jumped by .3% higher in December. The "food at home" category—which includes items you buy for cooking or eating at home—saw a staggering increase of 11.8% over the past 12 months.

Want to know how much it's affecting your favorite food items? Here's a quick rundown:

  • A carton of eggs is nearly 11.1% more expensive than a year ago.
  • Meats, poultry, fish, and eggs have seen a cost increase of 7.7%.
  • Dairy and related products have had the highest surge with a 15.3% price hike.

But don't worry, Youdle has got you covered! Smart shopping expert, Trae Bodge, has shared her top tips for saving on groceries. So, let's fight inflation together and keep those costs down at checkout.
