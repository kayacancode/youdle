7 Grocery Bargains Shoppers Love at Walmart

MEMPHIS, Tenn. (Youdle) – As the U.S. Department of Agriculture’s Economic Research Service reports a 23.6% increase in grocery prices from 2020 to 2024, American shoppers are feeling the pinch. Many are turning to Walmart for affordable grocery staples.

Shoppers and experts have highlighted seven items as the best value picks at Walmart:

  • Great Value Canned Goods: With affordable beans, broths, and canned fruits, your pantry becomes a savings palace. The fire-roasted tomatoes are a hit, often cheaper and tastier than other brands.
  • Syrup and Pancake Mix Combos: For a sweet, cost-effective breakfast, Walmart's combo pricing often beats other big box stores by $1-$2.
  • Meat Department Rollbacks: Bone-in chicken thighs are often under $1.25 per pound, perfect for budget-friendly meals like barbecue chicken and slow-cooker tacos.
  • Dairy Deals: While dairy prices can fluctuate, Walmart typically offers competitive prices.

These smart, cost-effective choices can help stretch your grocery budget amidst rising food costs. Check out Youdle for more grocery shopping tips and insights.
